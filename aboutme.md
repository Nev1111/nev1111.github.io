PANDAUDIT helps people across the finance field turn recurring spreadsheet work into clearer, reviewable data workflows.

This is for accountants, auditors, FP&A analysts, controllers, billing teams, revenue teams, treasury teams, operations analysts, students, and Excel power users who keep thinking: “there has to be a better way to do this.”

## What PANDAUDIT focuses on

<div class="pandaudit-card-grid fun-grid">
  <div class="pandaudit-card"><h3>Messy source files</h3><p>Exports, ledgers, billing reports, forecasts, workbooks, and operational data that do not arrive clean.</p></div>
  <div class="pandaudit-card"><h3>Reviewable workflows</h3><p>Summaries, exceptions, detail tabs, control totals, and notes that make review easier.</p></div>
  <div class="pandaudit-card"><h3>Finance judgment</h3><p>The point is not to remove judgment. The point is to focus judgment where it matters.</p></div>
  <div class="pandaudit-card"><h3>Fun learning</h3><p>Routes, challenges, field notes, and spreadsheet rescue missions instead of dry manuals.</p></div>
</div>

## The promise

PANDAUDIT starts with the real task:

- reconcile two reports
- clean an export
- prepare a workpaper
- explain a variance
- test a population
- refresh a recurring report
- package a dashboard input

Then it turns that task into a route someone else can understand.
