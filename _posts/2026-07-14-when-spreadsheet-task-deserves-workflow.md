Not every spreadsheet needs to become a formal workflow. Some tasks are one-time analyses. Others repeat often enough that they deserve structure.

## Standardize the task when it is:

- repeated monthly, quarterly, or every audit cycle
- reviewed by someone else
- tied to a control total
- based on similar source files each period
- prone to copy/paste errors
- hard to explain after the fact

## Keep it manual when it is:

- exploratory
- one-time
- judgment-heavy
- based on inconsistent source data
- too small to justify process design

## Accounting takeaway

The goal is not to automate everything. The goal is to standardize the work that repeats, matters, and needs review.
